Overview

Purpose

The website will help us to make the info available to our audience and to get sign up to get a ticket and/or membership. We will find a lot more people and help them to know about us and our services by looking the internet!.

Audience

We are looking for people of any gender between 16 to 57 years old to use our services. We will use words, colors, fonts and images that will fit this target audience. They will look for a clear message about our services, prices and membership options. They will be looking at the site from different devices like laptops, desktops and mobile devices.
